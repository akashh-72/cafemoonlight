Welcome to Moonlight

Nestled in the heart of Gadhinglaj, Moonlight Café is your go-to spot for delicious vegetarian bites, cozy vibes, and heartwarming moments. From handcrafted pasta and cheesy pizzas to your favorite coffee blends, we bring flavor and comfort together in one perfect place.

Whether you're here to chill, chat, or just recharge, we promise great taste and a great time—every single visit.
